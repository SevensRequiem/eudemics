# Preventive Health

Treatment infrastructure, substance health tracking, and genetic correction implementation.

## Universal Healthcare

Healthcare is free and universal. This is not a perk, it is infrastructure. A healthy population is a productive and capable population. A sick population is a drain on every other system.

## Substance Health Tracking

Tied to the substance credit system (see [[autonomy/liberty]]):
- Health baselines established at waiver signing
- Regular check-ins for moderate and high-tier substance users
- Escalating involvement as usage patterns indicate addiction
- Treatment always available and free: detox, counseling, medical support, narcan
- The goal is harm reduction, not punishment

## Genetic Correction

Implementation of the genetic improvement policy (see [[technology/ethics]]):
- Available at any stage: preconception, in utero, postnatal, adult
- Performed by vetted, experience-based node doctors
- Cross-node doctor requests honored
- Mandatory for known harmful mutations (with opt-out and feature consequences)
- Free for all citizens

## Cognitive Enhancement

Medical support for cognitive improvement available to all:
- Nootropics and neurofeedback where evidence supports them
- Brain-computer interfaces as they become safe and effective
- Nutritional and lifestyle optimization
- Integrated with the education system (see [[knowledge/education]])

The goal is to raise the floor. Not everyone will be a genius, but preventable cognitive limitations should be prevented.

## Treatment Over Prevention of Choice

The model provides treatment infrastructure, not prohibition. If someone uses substances and their health declines, the response is medical support, not punishment or withdrawal of care. You chose to use. The system chose to keep you alive and functional.
