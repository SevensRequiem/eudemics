# Law Creation

How laws are proposed, debated, and enacted.

## Git-Based Legislation

Laws are proposed, amended, and versioned like software. Every change is tracked, attributed, and reversible. The full history of every law is permanently visible.

## Process

1. Any competency-verified participant can propose legislation
2. The proposal enters debate, visible to all nodes
3. Evidence is gathered and evaluated (evidence-first, not ideology-first)
4. VRF-selected committee of nodes deliberates
5. Multi-node vote determines adoption
6. Enacted law is committed to the immutable record (see [[law/codification]])

There are no political parties, no lobbying, no campaign contributions. Policy is debated on evidence. See [[decision-making/consensus]].
