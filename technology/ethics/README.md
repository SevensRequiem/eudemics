# Technology Ethics

Boundaries for genetic improvement and other powerful technologies.

## Genetic Improvement

Eugenics is sound in principle. Improving the gene pool reduces suffering. The historical horror was never the principle, it was the implementation: murder, forced sterilization, racial pseudoscience.

This model uses CRISPR, gene therapy, and advanced medicine. Never murder. Never forced sterilization.

### Tiers of Intervention

| Category | Policy | Choice |
|----------|--------|--------|
| General defects and known harmful mutations | Automatic correction in germline. Species stability requires it. | Opt-out legal, but you lose access to some state features (similar to vaccine requirements). |
| Subjective traits with potential future harm | Research and predictive modeling. State advises based on evidence. | Not forced. The guiding hand. |
| Cosmetic/neutral traits (eye color, hair color, etc.) | No state opinion. | Entirely parent/self choice. |

"Needed corrections" means evidence-based, not aesthetic. A gene that causes Huntington's gets corrected. A gene that makes you short does not. The line is suffering versus difference.

### Opting Out

Opting out of needed genetic corrections is legal. But since these corrections are needed for species stability, opting out means opting out of some state features. The same principle as vaccine requirements: you are free to decline, and the system is free to adjust what it offers you.

### Race and Genetics

If research identifies genuine cognitive or physical disparities between populations, the response is CRISPR and medical improvement for affected populations, not discrimination. The goal is convergence upward. See [[foundations/human-nature]] for the broader position.

### Reproductive Context

Germline correction can happen at any stage. Node doctors (vetted, experience-based) perform it. Citizens can request cross-node doctors.

Natural births are fine, but the state educates on genetic correction benefits. Untracked births are mildly illegal because an untracked population outside the correction system risks a parallel society outside the system's protections and genetic improvements. Better to bring them in.

Some nodes may need to restrict birth rates (resource-constrained) or encourage them (population decline). Node-level policy within global guidelines, evidence-based.
