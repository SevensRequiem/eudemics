# Education

How people learn, improve, and advance through the competency system.

## Free and Generous

Education and medical support for cognitive improvement are free. This is not charity - it is the mechanism that prevents the competency system from becoming a permanent caste. If you gate participation by competency, you must make competency achievable for everyone.

## Not Memorization

Legacy schooling exists to produce workers. It optimizes for obedience, schedule compliance, and rote memorization. This system optimizes for actual capability.

What changes:
- Systems catered to group AND individual learning patterns (visual, kinesthetic, auditory, and deeper - how does this specific brain best absorb and integrate information)
- Computer science, critical thinking, and logic taught from early years
- Rights, liberties, and system literacy taught as core subjects
- Learning how to learn is as important as subject matter
- No forcing every child through identical curriculum at identical pace

## Age-Gated Learning Pipeline

| Age | Access and focus |
|-----|-----------------|
| 1-8 | Curated educational content only. Foundation building: language, basic reasoning, creativity, social skills. Internet is learning-only. |
| 9-13 | Expanded learning. More subjects, more depth. Access to broader knowledge bases. The 9-year window opens wider because evidence shows genuine genius can emerge this early. |
| 12+ | More unrestricted internet access. Still filtered for exploitation and harm. Competency assessment begins engaging in areas of demonstrated interest. |
| 16-18 | Near-full access. Formal competency tier system engages. Increasing autonomy in learning direction. |

These age gates are not arbitrary cutoffs. A 9-year-old who demonstrates exceptional capability gets access appropriate to that capability, not their birthday. The system is evidence-driven, not calendar-driven.

## Cognitive Improvement

Education is not just for children. Adults can:
- Re-take competency assessments at any time
- Access free courses, tutoring, and mentorship in any domain
- Receive medical support for cognitive enhancement (see [[health/preventive]])
- Request personalized learning plans based on their cognitive profile

The goal is continuous upward mobility. Your tier is never permanently fixed.

## Anti-Gaming

Assessment cannot be gamed through prep courses or memorization. See [[decision-making/participation]] for the detailed anti-gaming measures. The education system is designed to develop genuine capability, and the assessment system is designed to detect it. They reinforce each other.
