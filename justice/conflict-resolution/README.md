# Conflict Resolution

How disputes are adjudicated.

## Local Matters

Self-node plus neighbor nodes adjudicate local disputes. The judges/adjudicators are competency-verified. Evidence is evaluated, precedent is considered, and decisions are recorded in the immutable legal record.

## Major and Constitutional Matters

VRF cross-node committee replaces the supreme court concept. Randomly selected nodes, competency-verified, deliberate on constitutional questions and major disputes. VRF selection prevents manipulation of which nodes hear which cases.

## Legal System Evolution

The current legal system was sound 200 years ago. It needs massive expansion to close loopholes and integrate competency requirements. Much of US law, especially the Constitution, is applicable and should be expanded on, not discarded.

The Constitution's core principles (individual rights, separation of powers, due process) align with the model. What gets fixed: electoral corruption, lobbying, two-party stranglehold, lifetime appointments, and the loopholes that centuries of adversarial lawyering have carved.

## Adversarial Review

Any person subject to legal proceedings can request independent review from any node they choose, at state expense. The system does not assume its own infallibility.
