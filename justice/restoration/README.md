# Restoration

Rehabilitation over punishment, with honest acknowledgment of limits.

## Escalation Framework

### Tier 1: Guidance and Uplift
Low-level anti-social behavior (tribalism, minor conflicts, disrespect): education, counseling, community service. The goal is growth.

### Tier 2: Generous Containment
Serious offenses: containment facilities with dignity. Compartmentalized internet access (no propaganda, no chat, no porn - entertainment and knowledge only). Ongoing rehabilitation efforts.

Some offenders are victims of genetic anomalies. Blame is complicated. The system acknowledges this while still protecting the public.

### Tier 3: Experimental Rehabilitation
VR-based punishment and rehabilitation environments. Limited experimentation to find uplift pathways. The goal is still recovery if possible.

### Tier 4: Permanent Removal
Past a certain threshold, someone is a net drain on the system and an ongoing threat. The model acknowledges this tension honestly:

- Efficiency and safety say remove them
- But this creates a mechanism the state could abuse to disappear people
- **Safeguard:** removal requires multi-node VRF consensus, full public audit trail, and the person's complete record remains in the global data pool permanently. You can end someone. You cannot erase them. The record of why and how is permanent and auditable. If future review shows the removal was unjust, responsible officials face consequences.

This is not settled. It is a genuine tension between efficiency, safety, kindness, and preventing state abuse. The model states the tension rather than pretending it is resolved.

## Whistleblower Protection

Whistleblowers are protected by default in law. A whistleblower can choose ANY node for asylum and protection. That node is legally obligated to protect them.

This extends to general asylum seekers through the same mechanism. Protection means physical safety, legal defense, and preservation of their evidence in the global data pool.

## Guardianship Process

When the justice system determines guardianship is needed (see [[autonomy/rights]] for criteria), the dual guardianship model activates: state plus loved ones, with all the safeguards described in [[health/mental]].
