# Enforcement

How rules are upheld and how the system handles those who break them.

## Competency-Verified Enforcement

Law enforcement personnel are competency-verified and heavily vetted. The current model of minimally trained officers with broad authority is inadequate.

Enforcement is proportional: minor infractions get minor responses. Escalation follows the restoration framework (see [[justice/restoration]]).

## Loophole Closure

Laws are continuously reviewed and updated to close loopholes. The adversarial legal tradition created centuries of exploitable gaps. The model actively patches these through evidence-based legislative review.
