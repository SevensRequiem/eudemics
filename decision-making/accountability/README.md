# Accountability

How the system prevents corruption and ensures transparency.

## Cross-Node Auditing

Any citizen or node can request re-evaluation or audit of any other node at any time. The audit is performed by randomly selected nodes (VRF selection prevents collusion).

This is not adversarial - it is hygiene. Regular audits are normal system maintenance, not accusations. Nodes that resist audit face escalating scrutiny.

## AI Coup Prevention

Traitor determination must ALWAYS have human-in-the-loop. If AI can unilaterally flag traitors, a rogue or weaponized AI could systematically remove humans from power.

Rule: AI can analyze patterns and recommend investigation. The actual determination of treason requires a human VRF committee vote. No exceptions. Even if slower. Speed is not worth the existential risk of AI-controlled purges.

This extends to all consequential decisions: AI advises, humans decide. The system may evolve as trust in AI governance builds (see [[technology/automation]]), but the default is human authority with AI assistance.

## Treason

Genuine traitors whose actions are evidence-supported as detrimental to the whole or its security face severe punishment. This extends to civilians in sensitive assigned positions. The system's security is existential - undermining it endangers everyone.

Evidence standard is high: documented harm, not just disagreement or incompetence. Whistle-blowing is explicitly NOT treason (see [[justice/restoration]] for whistleblower protection).

## Immutable Records

All governance decisions, votes, audits, and their outcomes are recorded in the append-only legal record (see [[law/codification]]). RFC 3161 timestamped. No node majority can vote to erase the record.

Historical revisionism is unconstitutional. See [[law/constitutional]].
